About Us

Furniture Assist started out in 2004 as a tiny outreach of St. Luke and All Saints Episcopal Church in Union, NJ, but has grown into a full-fledged Non-Profit Corporation that aids 15 clients a week. Since becoming an official non-profit in 2006, we have become active helpers in the outreach efforts of over 100 organizations. Thanks to the help of thousands of families, we have assisted thousands of clients.

But we can grow much more, even while staying in our 24 Commerce Street, Springfield location. Furniture Assist has the current capacity to aid 60 to 90 clients a week, but is limited by the number of volunteers and donations. With your help, we can make a real change in the community we all share.
