Referring a Client

If your agency is already approved and is ready to refer a client, please use the following procedure:

  1. Complete and fax to us the client referral form. Within a few weeks, we will fax to you a signed form with an appointment date.
  2. Inform the client of the appointment date, provide them with a printed copy of our procedures, and give them directions to our distribution center.
  3. Contact us if the client is not available for their assigned appointment date.
  4. Ensure that the client understands that:
    • They will only be admitted on the day and time of their appointment.
    • Young children are not allowed, and must be left home.
    • Furniture is distributed on a first-come, first-serve basis, within reason.
    • We cannot store furniture for later pick-up.
    • There is no guarantee that we will have any given item. High demand items like beds are in short supply.
    • If the client is going to miss an appointment, they must call us before their scheduled arrival time.
    • It may be a few weeks before the client receives an appointment date.
If you call concerning the status of your client, please be sure to leave their name so we can respond directly. All communications are held in strict confidence.

Re-Stocking Clothes Closets

If your agency is already approved and has its own clothes closet, you can schedule an appointment to re-stock your closet any Monday or Wednesday between the hours of 10:00 AM and 2:00 PM. You must bring your own transportation and bags for the clothes. Clothes distributions are made by appointment only. Don't bring clients when you re-stock your closet. They should select from the clothes closet back at your office.

Assisting Clients

In the event of snow or some other emergency, please ask your client to call us at 908-868-6007, extension 6, to defer their appointment. Check our twitter feed or the home page of this website after 8:00 AM, where we will post a message if we will be closed due to weather or a holiday. If we are open despite snow or an emergency, your client must call to confirm that they will not be there.
