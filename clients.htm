Becoming a Client

In order to receive furniture items from Furniture Assist, you must be referred by one of our approved social agencies. Show this website to your social agency and ensure they are approved with us. Then, your social agency can arrange an appointment to come to our warehouse location at 24 Commerce Street, Springfield, New Jersey, 07081, between 9:00 AM and noon, Sundays. All furniture items are provided for free, but you must provide your own transportation.

To find out if your agency is approved, visit our approved agencies list.
