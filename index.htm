Donate to Furniture Assist now and make a difference.

We receive second-hand furniture and give it to those in difficult positions in their lives — people who have been left without furniture by a sudden house fire, a tough divorce, or poverty. From those who have, to those who need.

You can find us at 24 Commerce Street, Springfield, New Jersey, 07081. We're open for furniture donations every Sunday, with the exception of certain holidays, from noon to 2:30 PM.
