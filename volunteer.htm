Volunteering

Furniture Assist is an all-volunteer organization, and we are always looking for more volunteers! Our volunteers decide when they work and how they can help. We also provide community service credit upon request. Furniture Assist volunteers assist in all parts of the organization, which include:

  • Helping clients select the items they need
  • Recieving, inspecting, and storing donations
  • Participating as a Board Member. Board Members are responsible for grant requests, fundraising events, social media, and other activities
